Analytics Label enables you to group analytics of different notification events or filter analytics of a single notification event. All labels values must be configured here.

To enable it,

1. Go to Workspace Settings
2. Select Add Ons
3. Click 'Configure' in Analytics Label card.

## How to add analytics label

In the analytics label page, you can add up to **16 labels**. You can click 'Add Label' for adding new labels. Once you have added all the labels, click 'Save Changes'.
