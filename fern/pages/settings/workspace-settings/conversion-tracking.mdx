Conversation Tracking enables you to track how many users did the desired action after sending the notification.

To enable it,

1. Go to Workspace Settings
2. Select Add Ons
3. Enable the toggle in Conversation Tracking.

Once its enabled, you will be able to see the Conversion tracking option in notification event page. You can read about it [here](./event-creation#4-track-conversion-optional)
