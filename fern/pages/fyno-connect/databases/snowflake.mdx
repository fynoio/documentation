To connect Fyno Connect with your Snowflake database:

<Steps>
  <Step>Navigate to **Databases** and click on Snowflake.</Step>
  <Step>
    In the pop-up, enter the following details.
    <ul>
      <li>custom name - Unique name to identify your snowflake connection in Fyno.</li>
      <li>accountname - Refer this [doc](https://docs.snowflake.com/en/user-guide/admin-account-identifier) to learn how to identify or retrieve the account name from your Snowflake account.</li>
      <li>username</li>
      <li>password</li>
      <li>warehousename</li>
      <li>dbname</li>
    </ul>
  </Step>
  <Step>Click on "Add Database" to test the connection and successfully save it. After the database is added successfully, you will see it listed on the same page under **Connected DBs**.</Step>
  <Step>You can now create cohorts from the data in your database. See [Create Cohorts.](./create-cohort)</Step>
</Steps>
