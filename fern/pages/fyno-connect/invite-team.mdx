**Team** is a structure that Fyno built for you to work and collaborate seamlessly with your colleagues.

## The Need for Teams

**Teams** allows you to:

- Have a centralized view of the team members along with their access levels and other details
- Collaborate easily with colleagues on projects and tasks within the Fyno Connect.

## Creating and Viewing Team Members

To create a new team member, follow the below steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>From your Fyno Connect account, click on the Profile icon from top right corner and select **Workspace Settings** from the list of options.</Step>
  <Step>In the Team page, click on the **Invite User** button, fill in the email ID and click on submit.</Step>
  <Step>The newly invited colleague will receive an email with details to sign up on the Fyno Connect.</Step>
  <Step>Owner can invite a user and give them either Workspace Admin or Developer or Support role.</Step>
  <Step>A Workspace Admin can invite a user and give them either Developer or Support role.</Step>
</Steps>

## Deleting a Team Member

To delete a team member from your workspace, follow the below steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>From your Fyno Connect account, click on the Profile icon from top right corner and select **Workspace Settings** from the list of options.</Step>
  <Step>On the page that opens, click on the "more" menu of the name that you would like to remove from the workspace and select **Delete**. You will be prompted to **Confirm** your action and that's it!</Step>
</Steps>

<Info>Every Workspace can have only one Owner.</Info>
