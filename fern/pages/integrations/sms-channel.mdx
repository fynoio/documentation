## Fyno's SMS Simulator

We know you love testing and to provide this feature for you, with realtime logs and experience of configuration, Fyno has come up with the **SMS Simulator** to help you do just that! Avoid the hassle of integrating your providers and use our **SMS Simulator** to get an idea of how an integration would work when added to Fyno's platform.

To set it up, follow the below steps:

<Steps>

    <Step>From the left navigation panel, click on **Integrations**.</Step>

    <Step>
        On the page that opens, under the SMS section, find and click on
        **Simulator**.
    </Step>

    <Step>
        In the pop-up that appears, provide a name and click on **Add Account**.
    </Step>

</Steps>

And you are all set to go!

### Benefits of using Fyno's SMS Simulator

-   You are not using any of your live providers
-   You will save credits from your live account when testing
-   You will save usage bandwidth on your live providers
