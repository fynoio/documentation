> 👍 Fyno's SMS Simulator
>
> We know you love testing.
>
> And to provide this feature for you, with realtime logs and experience of configuration, Fyno has come up with the **SMS Simulator** to help you do just that! Avoid the hassle of integrating your providers and use our **SMS Simulator**to get an idea of how an integration would work when added to Fyno's platform.
>
> To set it up, follow the below steps:
>
> 1. From the left navigation panel, click on **Integrations**.
> 2. On the page that opens, under the SMS section, find and click on **Simulator**.
> 3. In the pop-up that appears, provide a name and click on **Add Account**.
>
> And you are all set to go!
>
> #### Benefits of using Fyno's SMS Simulator
>
> -   You are not using any of your live providers
> -   You will save credits from your live account when testing
> -   You will save usage bandwidth on your live providers
