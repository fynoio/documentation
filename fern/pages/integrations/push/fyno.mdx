**Fyno Push** combines 3 different push notification services, in one plugin. You can integrate one or all of them to get the best possible outcome in terms of push notification delivery.

<Tip title="Minimum requirements!">You will need to install and configure Google FCM as default/primary provider, if nothing else, to make Fyno Push functional.</Tip>
