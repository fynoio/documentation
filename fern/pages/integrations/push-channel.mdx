## Fyno's Push Simulator

We know you love testing and to provide this feature for you, with realtime logs and experience of configuration, Fyno has come up with the **Push Simulator** to help you do just that! Avoid the hassle of integrating your providers and use our **Push Simulator** to get an idea of how an integration would work when added to Fyno's platform.

To set it up, follow the below steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>From the left navigation panel, click on **Integrations**.</Step>
  <Step>On the page that opens, under the Push section, find and click on **Simulator**.</Step>
  <Step>In the pop-up that appears, provide a name, and dummy information in the other fields, and click on **Add Account**.</Step>
</Steps>

And you are all set to go!

### Benefits of using Fyno's Push Simulator

- You are not using any of your live providers
- You will save credits from your live account when testing
- You will save usage bandwidth on your live providers
