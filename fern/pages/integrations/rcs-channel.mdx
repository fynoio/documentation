RCS, or Rich Communication Services, enhances traditional SMS messaging by offering a suite of service capabilities, including group chat, file transfers, typing notifications, and more. This evolution in messaging is driven by the desire to enrich user engagement and communication experiences, both for personal and business interactions. The overall vision of RCS is to provide subscribers and enterprises with additional communication options.

Businesses can leverage RCS for marketing, customer engagement, and event communication by offering rich, interactive messaging experiences in a branded environment. Success stories highlight increased customer engagement and conversion rates compared to traditional channels.
