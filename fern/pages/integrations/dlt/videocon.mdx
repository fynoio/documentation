Before we get started, you will need to have a Videocon DLT account already set up.

1. Navigate to Integrations -> DLT.
2. Click on the "Videocon" button.
3. In the below pop-up, fill in:
   ![](https://fynodocs.s3.ap-south-1.amazonaws.com/images/dlt_vodofone_config.png)

    1. **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.
    2. **DLT Provider**: 'Videocon' is pre-filled.
    3. **PE ID**: Enter the Private Entity Id (PE Id) from Videocon DLT platform
