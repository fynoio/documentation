<Note>Applicable only if your business is operating in India</Note>

Before we get started, you will need to have a Videocon DLT account already set up.

<Steps>
### Find the Provider
Navigate to Integrations -> DLT. Click on the "Videocon" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the below pop-up, fill in:
![](/images/dlt_vodofone_config.png)

- **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.
- **DLT Provider**: 'Videocon' is pre-filled.
- **PE ID**: Enter the Private Entity Id (PE Id) from Videocon DLT platform

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>
