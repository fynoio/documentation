To setup a Airtel DLT account already set up, follow the below steps .

1. Navigate to Integrations -> DLT.
2. Click on the "Airtel DLT" button.
3. In the below pop-up, fill in:
   ![](/images/dlt_airtel_integration.png)

    1. **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.
    2. **DLT Provider**: 'Airtel DLT' is pre-filled.
    3. **PE ID**: PE ID (Personal Entity id) can be fetched from the dashboard as shown below.
       ![](/images/dlt_airtel_config.png)
