To setup a Airtel DLT account already set up, follow the below steps .

<Steps>
### Find the Provider
Navigate to Integrations -> DLT. Click on the "Airtel DLT" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the below pop-up, fill in:
![](/images/dlt_airtel_integration.png)

- **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.
- **DLT Provider**: 'Airtel DLT' is pre-filled.
- **PE ID**: PE ID (Personal Entity id) can be fetched from the dashboard as shown below.
  ![](/images/dlt_airtel_config.png)

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>
