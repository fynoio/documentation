Before we get started, you will need an active WhatsApp number as well as templates pre-configured in your Interakt account

<Steps>
### Find the Provider
Navigate to the Integrations page. Under "WhatsApp" section, click on the "Interakt" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the pop-up that appears, fill in:

- **Custom name:** Provide a name to help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.
- **API Key:** In your Interakt Account, under the Developer Setting, you will find the find 'Secret Key'.
  ![](/images/whatsapp_interakt_apikey.png)

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>

## Message Delivery Status

While Fyno has the capacity to track the notification delivery status, Interakt requires a manual update of the Fyno Callback Endpoint in order to receive these reports. To update the Callback manually follow these steps:

<Steps>
  <Step>Log into your Interakt Account</Step>
  <Step>From the top right corner, find **Developer Setting** and click on **Configure** under Configure Webhook.</Step>
  <Step>On the page that loads, enter the Callback URL provided in the popup in Webhook URL. Enter the Secret Key. ![](/images/whatsapp_interakt_callback.png)</Step>
  <Step>Under **Template Messages sent via API** and **Template Message sent via Interakt Campaigns**, select all the required options and click Submit.</Step>
  <Step>Test your integration by sending a message from the Fyno App. You will be able to see this in the the logs, when you click on it, under the **[Delivery](./log-details-delivery)** tab.</Step>
</Steps>
