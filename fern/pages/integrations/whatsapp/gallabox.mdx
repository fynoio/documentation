Before we get started, you will need an active WhatsApp number as well as templates pre-configured in your Gallabox account.

<Steps>
### Find the Provider
Navigate to the Integrations page. Under "WhatsApp" section, click on the "Gallabox" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the pop-up, fill in:

-   **Custom name**: Provide a name to help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.

-   **Access Token**: In your Gallabox account, in the left navigation, Integrations -> Configuration and copy the Access Token
    ![](/images/whatsapp_gallabox_accesstoken.png)

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>

## Message Delivery Status

While Fyno has the capacity to track the notification delivery status, Gallabox requires a manual update of the Fyno Callback Endpoint in order to receive these reports. To update the Callback manually follow these steps:

<Steps>
    <Step>Log in to your Gallabox account.</Step>
    <Step> the left navigation pane, find and click on **Integrations**.</Step>
    <Step>
        On the page that loads, under Configuration tab, add callback URL (you
        can get it from the integration popup) in Status URL field.
    </Step>
</Steps>
