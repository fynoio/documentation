Before we get started, you will need to have a India SMS account already set up, along with SenderID and Templates.

<Steps>

### Find the Provider

Navigate to the Integrations page. Under "SMS" section, click on the "India SMS" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the pop-up, fill in:

-   **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.

-   **API UserName**: Enter the username you use to log into India SMS portal

-   **API Password**: Enter the password you use to log into India SMS portal

-   **Sender**: The Sender that is being used by you to send out messages.

-   **Type of Route**: Select the type of route based on the configuration added in India SMS application.

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>

## Message Delivery Status

While Fyno has the capacity to track the notification delivery status, India SMS requires a manual update of the Fyno Callback Endpoint in order to receive these reports. To update the Callback manually follow these steps:

<Steps>

    <Step>Log into your India SMS Account.</Step>

    <Step>
        Configure the URL given in the India SMS Integration popup and Save.
    </Step>

</Steps>
