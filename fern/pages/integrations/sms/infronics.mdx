Before we get started, you will need to have a Infronics account already set up, along with SenderID and Templates.

<Steps>

### Find the Provider

Navigate to the Integrations page. Under "SMS" section, click on the "Infronics" button.

### Configure the Integration

In the pop-up, fill in:

-   **Custom name**: Provide a name that would help you identify the configured account in Fyno's portal.

-   **Username**: The username that is displayed on your Infronics Account when you log in and view the dashboard

-   **Password**: Password used to log into the Customer ID entered in the previous field

-   **Sender**: Enter the Sender ID that you want to use.

-   **DLT Entity Id**: Enter your DLT registered Entity ID

-   **Route**: Select the type of route from the below option
    -   Transactional
    -   OTP

### Complete the Integration

Click on "Add Account" once done, and you are all set!

</Steps>

## Message Delivery Status

Infronics, by default, provides the status of the message without any additional setup needed.

Once the above integration is complete, the status of the notification, which will be shared by Infronics will be displayed in the logs, when you click on it, under the **[Delivery](./log-details-delivery)** tab.
